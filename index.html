Who We Are

Built on Responsibility & Results

Tradenlogic is a UAE-registered Free Zone Company (FZC) specialising in the full spectrum of waste management services — from collection and recycling to scrap metal trading and structural demolition.

We work with businesses across the industrial, construction, and commercial sectors, providing compliant, efficient, and environmentally responsible solutions that reduce costs and environmental impact simultaneously.

Our team brings deep industry knowledge and operational excellence to every project — whether it's a single scrap collection or a full-scale building demolition and material recovery operation.
